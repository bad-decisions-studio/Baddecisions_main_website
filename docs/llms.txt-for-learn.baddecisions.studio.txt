## Curriculum (22 modules · 20+ hours · 55+ practical lessons)

- **Module 1 — Getting Started**: Installation, interface navigation, project setup, and visual story planning
- **Module 2 — Modeling Tools**: Modeling basics, advanced tools, shaping objects with precision
- **Module 3 — Scene Building**: Importing assets, modular environment building, layout tools
- **Module 4 — Materials & Textures**: Basics, advanced techniques, AI texture workflows
- **Module 5 — Lighting**: Basic and advanced lighting, glowing surfaces, cinematic mood
- **Module 6 — MetaHuman**: Creating characters, clothing and props, animation retargeting, AI face animation
- **Module 7 — Animation**: Animating characters, timing control, syncing with environment
- **Module 8 — Motion Design Tools**: Animated materials and visual effects
- **Module 9 — Cloth Simulation**: Dynamic clothing and accessories with Unreal's built-in tools
- **Module 10 — Optimization**: Nanite and performance workflows for efficient cinematic worlds
- **Module 11 — Blueprints**: Interactive actors and reusable logic
- **Module 12 — Sequencer**: Animating scenes, layering actions, Master Sequencer
- **Module 13 — Cinematic Camera Tools**: Rig rails, cranes, cinematic framing
- **Module 14 — Virtual Camera**: Use an iPhone or Android phone to control cameras and record actions in real time
- **Module 15 — Decals**: Detail work for photorealism
- **Module 16 — Foliage**: Plant thousands of trees and grass with the Foliage tool
- **Module 17 — Render Settings**: Photorealistic exports with Lumen
- **Module 18 — HDRI**: Realistic skies and image-based environments
- **Module 19 — Niagara Particles**: Particle systems and FX with Niagara
- **Module 20 — AI Workflows**: Latest AI models for animation, texture, and 3D model generation
- **Module 21 — Chaos Destruction**: Real-time shattering, breaking, and exploding
- **Module 22 — Title Sequence**: Crafted cinematic title sequence
