> Become the #1 option in your industry. Hands-on AI workflows, curated tool picks, and step-by-step playbooks to help you save time, win clients, and actually get ahead. Built by Bad Decisions Studio.

The Ultimate AI Program is the flagship AI education product from Bad Decisions Studio. It teaches the AI workflows that get you hired and paid — practical playbooks and curated tool picks designed to help anyone (no technical background required) become irreplaceable. The thesis: you're not behind because you're not smart enough; you're behind because nobody gave you a clear, practical place to start. There are a thousand tools, a million tutorials, and zero roadmaps — this is the roadmap.

The program is structured around three pillars: **Foundations** (how to think), **Toolbox** (how to use), and **Playbooks** (how to build).

## About Bad Decisions Studio

Bad Decisions is a creative and media studio that delivers projects across the US, Canada, Europe, and the Middle East. Studio content has reached millions and led to partnerships with brands like Dell, Epic Games, and Autodesk. The team has been building in this space long before AI entered the picture — when it did, it didn't replace what they do, it supercharged it. The studio integrated AI into existing workflows and saw what it could do for speed, quality, and profitability. The result: more clients, better work. The studio has already trained 1,500+ students through its Unreal Engine program and built a community of 540,000+ followers. Now applying the same approach to AI.

Run by Farhad Shababi and Faraz Shababi and a team from Vancouver and Dubai. The studio operates across three pillars: Content (a podcast, long-form educational videos, and short-form videos), Education (premium programs and free tutorials), and Services (consulting, AI-enabled solutions, training, interactive experiences, content strategy).
